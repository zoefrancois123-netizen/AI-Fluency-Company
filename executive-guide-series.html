AI governance is not a single document. It is three layers.

Most organisations treat AI governance as a policy problem. It is not. It is a stack: the framework, the lifecycle, and the people. Without all three, governance is either theoretical or unworkable.

This series gives executive teams the complete picture. Each volume stands alone, but together they answer the three questions every board should be asking: what do we govern, how do we govern it, and who is capable of doing it.
